This asset contains 150+ low poly environment models(Trees, Mountains, Rocks etc) and demo scene.

-Buildings (19)
-Ground (4)
-Mountains (40)
-Rocks (25)
-Statues (29)
-Trees (44)
-Etc (32)


This asset is optimized for mobile performance.
Applied POT texture atlas.
Few tris & verts.

You can easily add these models to your game.

Desert, Winter, Volcano, Forest, Fantasy atmosphere.


-


This asset package was made with Unity version 5.35 and doesn't have any standard assets.

A texture atlas is included which contains textures for all models(189 preferb files) and a map(4 prefab files) of demo scene.
